About me

 May 5, 2018
Welcome to the blog! I’m Hugo, my background is in Psychology, more specifically, the area of face perception. Always eager to learn and after falling in love with R, I will blog mostly about data related topics(e.g. importing, wrangling, visualization, statistics). Not yet Machine Learning, I’m still learning. I want to share what I have been learning in R and try to help others to work with it. I also enjoy other things, non-data related, like history, tvshows, and literature, which I might blog about it.
